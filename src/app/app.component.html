Animation Timing

There are three properties that can be used in animation timing

  • Delay - the delay before the commencement of the animation
  • Duration - the length of the animation. Duration and delay can be declared in milliseconds '400', a string in milliseconds '400ms' or a string in seconds '0.4s'
  • Easing - the change in speed of the animation as it occurs

These are all coding in the 'timing string', which has a format such as '0.8s 500ms ease-in'. In this example, the animation will have a duration of 0.8 seconds, a delay of half a second and progressively speed up as it runs.
